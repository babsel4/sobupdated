HOW TO BUY?

  1. Create an SPL wallet using sollet.io
  2. Go to a DEX either Dexlab or Raydium
  3. Get USDC to your wallet
  4. Click + sign to add custom market
  5. Paste the Market ID and other details
  6. Swap the USDC for SOB
  7. Hodl you SOB for future rewards
